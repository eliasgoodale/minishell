Processes:
			> exist independently 
            > an instance of a computer program that is being executed
            > contains: program code, and current activity
            > a process can be made up of multiple threads of execution
			> processes interact through IPC mechanisms of the OS

Threads: 
			> smallest sequence of programmed instructionions that can be managed indepedently by a scheduler
			> typically exist as subsets of a process
			> threads of a process share memory in an address space
